About Ingrevo

Revolutionizing how you shop for safer, healthier products

The Problem We Solve

Shoppers in hypermarkets struggle to understand product ingredients and allergen information. Labels are small, technical, and often in English only — creating health risks, especially for people with allergies, dietary restrictions, or health conditions.

Our Solution

Ingrevo combines smart kiosks, intelligent trolleys, and AI-powered scanning to give you instant, clear information about every product you pick up. Get real-time allergen alerts, personalized health recommendations, and safer alternatives — all in your preferred language.
