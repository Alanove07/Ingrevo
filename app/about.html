About Ingrevo

Empowering safer shopping decisions through intelligent ingredient analysis

Our Mission

At Ingrevo, we believe everyone deserves to shop with confidence. Our mission is to make ingredient and allergen information instantly accessible, helping millions of people make informed decisions about what they consume.

Using cutting-edge AI technology, we analyze thousands of products to provide real-time allergen alerts, health scores, and personalized recommendations tailored to your dietary needs.
