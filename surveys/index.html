About This Survey

Welcome to the Ingrevo Voice of Stakeholders (VOS) Survey Program. Your feedback is crucial in helping us develop an intelligent system that promotes safer product choices and allergen awareness in hypermarkets.

Why Your Input Matters:

  • Shape the development of AI-powered product safety features
  • Help create better allergen alert systems
  • Improve ingredient transparency for everyone
  • Contribute to a safer shopping experience
